Welcome to Nova – A Brazilian manufacturer and direct importer of South American hardwoods. We specialize in tropical wood products including exterior decking, interior flooring, rough lumber and industrial programs.

With four offices in Brazil – Curitiba, Belem, Manaus and Ji-Parana – Nova offers the very best in quality control, logistics, product knowledge and supplier relationships. Nova also works with the leading manufacturers in South America to give our customers superior, top-quality products at the most competitive prices available.
